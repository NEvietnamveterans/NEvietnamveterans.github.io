    Chapter 279 Omaha, Nebraska

      Chapter 279 is the largest in Nebraska with over 200 members. Meetings are held on the second Wednesday of every month at 1830 hours. Location is 13913 S Steet in the Millard Legion Post 374. All Vietnam Veterans and spouses are invited to attend. For additional information contact Terry Hester, President - 402 677-2316 or rterryhester@cox.net

    Chapter 455 Falls City, Nebraska

    Chapter 727 Lincoln, Nebraska

      Chapter 727 is in Lincoln, Nebraska. Meetings are held on the fourth Friday of every month at the VFW Post 3606 on West A and Fourth Street. For additional information contact: Greg Holloway, President - 402 803-9180 or gregholloway46@gmail.com
