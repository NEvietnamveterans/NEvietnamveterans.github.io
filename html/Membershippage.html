    Vietnam Veterans of America Chapter 279 - Membership

    The chapter has 190 members and 10 associate's. As a member you can become as active as your time, talents and interests allow you.

    All Vietnam Veterans are invited to attend our meetings and to become a member of our Chapter.  As a member of Chapter 279, you can become as active as your time, talents, and interests allow you.

    Membership includes a subscription to VVA national award-winning newspaper, The VVA Veteran, bringing you updates on issues and legislation affecting veterans, as well as unique articles on the people, places, and history of the Vietnam experience.


    How To Join Vietnam Veterans of America Chapter 279

    Membership is open to any U.S. Armed Forces Veteran who served on active duty(for other than training purposes) in the Republic of Vietnam between Februarys 28, 1961 and May 7, 1975, or in any duty location between August 5, 1964 and May 7, 1975. If you are on the Vietnam Veterans at large list and would like to join a local chapter, contact one of the chapter officers.
